This is
dAIly

an AI powered journaling application

our project

A journaling app which performs emotion recognition on your journal entries and generates images based on the events and emotions that occurred throughout the day. Keeps the journal as an album of images. This way the user can analyze and visualize their journal based on images generated. Daily also provides anonymous journal sharing and reading features to improve user interactivity. User can check their journaling statistics provided by our application and NLP model, which gives an edge to our application compared to standard journaling application.
